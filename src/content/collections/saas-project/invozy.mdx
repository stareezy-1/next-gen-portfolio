Invozy is a payment-aware invoicing and collections SaaS for the Indonesian market, built and operated solo. Founder and sole engineer. Currently in active development (Phase 1 MVP).

## What it does

- Invoice builder with a **live A4 preview** and reusable templates
- **Multi-channel delivery** — email + WhatsApp in one click
- **Smart Collections** — automated D-3 / D-1 / D+1 / D+7 reminders that cancel on payment
- Status tracking: **Draft → Sent → Viewed → Paid**, with a public invoice link
- Company profile, brand assets, staff roles (Owner / Staff), and an append-only audit log

## Planned (Phase 2)

DOKU pay-in links so clients pay invoices directly, an active balance, and bank payout via Kirim DOKU — plus Free / Pro / Business subscriptions and a referral program, reusing Lyra's production payment stack.

## Stack

Next.js · Hono on Cloudflare Workers · D1 + Drizzle · KV · R2 · Queues · Resend · DOKU (planned).
