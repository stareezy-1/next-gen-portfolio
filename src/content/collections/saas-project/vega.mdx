Vega is a single-client TikTok multi-distribution platform, built and operated solo. Sole engineer. Currently in active development (pending TikTok App Review).

## What it does

- **Mass distribution** — one video to all accounts, a group, or a manual selection (up to 500)
- **Scheduling** up to 365 days ahead, with real-time job status
- **Centralized analytics** — views, likes, shares, comments polled every 6 hours
- **Compliant by design** — official TikTok Content Posting + Data APIs, OAuth 2.0 + PKCE, no bots or scraping
- Invite-only auth (Google + Magic Link), RBAC, encrypted tokens, append-only audit log

## Stack

Next.js 15 · Hono on Cloudflare Workers · D1 + Drizzle · R2 · Queues · Cron · a Fly.io upload worker · TikTok SDK · Better Auth.
